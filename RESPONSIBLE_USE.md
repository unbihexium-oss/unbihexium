# Responsible Use Policy

## Purpose

Unbihexium is designed for civilian applications in Earth observation, environmental monitoring, urban planning, agriculture, and scientific research. This document outlines guidelines for responsible use.

## Intended Applications

This library is designed for:

- Environmental monitoring and conservation
- Agricultural analysis and crop management
- Urban planning and infrastructure assessment
- Scientific research and education
- Disaster response and humanitarian aid
- Climate change analysis
- Natural resource management

## Prohibited Uses

Users must NOT use this library for:

- Development of weapons or military systems
- Illegal surveillance of individuals
- Activities violating local, national, or international law
- Human rights violations
- Environmental destruction

## Detection Models

The object detection capabilities in this library are provided as neutral frameworks. The "military objects detection" capability is implemented as a configurable detection framework that can be adapted for various domains. Users are responsible for:

- Ensuring appropriate use of detection models
- Configuring models for legitimate purposes only
- Complying with all applicable regulations

## Export Controls

Some capabilities may be subject to export control regulations. Users are responsible for:

- Understanding applicable export control laws
- Obtaining necessary licenses or approvals
- Ensuring compliance with all regulations

## Data Privacy

When processing satellite imagery:

- Ensure you have proper authorization for imagery use
- Respect privacy regulations in your jurisdiction
- Do not process imagery in ways that violate individual privacy
